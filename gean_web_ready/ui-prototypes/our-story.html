The GEAN Story

Born from a

01
English

GEAN was born from a simple but powerful belief: that the professionals who work hardest deserve products that work just as hard for them. We saw hands that were overworked, dried out, and ignored — and we decided to change that.

Our journey began in the heart of Bangkok, where a team of skincare scientists and designers came together with one mission: to create the perfect hand cream for the modern office environment. Zero greasiness, instant absorption, and a scent engineered to promote focus and calm.

Every ingredient in GEAN is chosen with purpose. From 95% Squalane Extract to Damask Rose, we source only the finest materials that meet our strict standards of efficacy and purity. No shortcuts, no compromises — because your skin deserves nothing less than the best.

GEAN is more than a hand cream. It is a daily ritual, a moment of self-care in the middle of your busiest day. A sanctuary for your hands — and a reminder that you deserve to feel good while doing great work.
