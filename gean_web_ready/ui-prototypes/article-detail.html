Why Squalane is the Gold Standard for Hand Hydration

20 May 2026 5 min read Skincare Science

In the crowded world of skincare ingredients, few have earned as much acclaim — or as much scientific backing — as Squalane. Originally derived from shark liver oil (and now sustainably sourced from sugarcane), this lightweight, odourless oil has become the cornerstone of premium moisturising formulas worldwide.

"Squalane is the closest thing to our skin's own natural sebum, which is why it absorbs so quickly and feels completely invisible." — GEAN Research Team

What Makes Squalane Different?

Unlike many moisturisers that sit on top of the skin or leave a greasy residue, Squalane penetrates deeply into the epidermis. Its molecular structure — nearly identical to our skin's natural oils — means it's recognised and accepted by the skin barrier without triggering excess sebum production.

For hand care specifically, this matters enormously. Hands are washed more frequently than any other body part, constantly stripping away their natural protective oils. Squalane works to replenish what's lost, restoring suppleness and preventing the micro-cracks that lead to roughness and irritation.

The GEAN Difference: 95% Squalane

Most hand creams on the market contain Squalane at concentrations between 1–5%. GEAN's formulation uses 95% Squalane Extract as the primary active ingredient — a concentration that delivers visible results from the first application.

Combined with our Ceramide Complex and D-Panthenol, the Squalane in GEAN works synergistically to not only hydrate but to repair and fortify the skin barrier. The result is hands that feel soft immediately, and stay soft long after the cream has absorbed.

Zero Greasiness: The Professional Promise

One of the most common complaints about hand creams is the greasy, slippery feeling that makes typing or using touch screens impossible. Squalane's unique lightweight texture eliminates this problem entirely. GEAN absorbs within seconds, leaving hands silky but never sticky — the perfect companion for a demanding workday.

Whether you're in back-to-back meetings, working at a keyboard, or shaking hands with a client, GEAN works invisibly so you can focus on what matters most.
