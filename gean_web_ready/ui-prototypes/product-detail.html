Usage

The ritual

01
Apply

02

Work the cream in with slow, deliberate strokes. Pay attention to the cuticles and knuckles — the areas most prone to dryness and neglect.

03
Continue

Twenty seconds to full absorption. No residue. No disruption. Return to your keyboard, your meeting, your day — hands quietly restored.
